## Arqus International Innovators Awards 2024

Living Chess was selected as one of the finalists of the **Arqus International Innovators Award 2024**, representing the University of Wrocław. The project was listed under the title *Intertwining Chess with Theatre*.

The project was presented during the finals, showcasing its combination of strategic gameplay and performative storytelling to a European academic and entrepreneurial audience.

### What was presented

The presentation highlighted the project's early-stage potential, its innovative approach to collaborative, narrative-driven gameplay, and its relevance to interdisciplinary, "born-global" creative initiatives.

Key aspects covered:

- The role-based structure where every participant controls one piece
- The three-stage decision cycle: suggestion → curation → weighted vote
- The King's unique position: curating without voting
- The research instrument potential for studying group decision-making

### About the Arqus Alliance

The Arqus Alliance is a European University Alliance committed to advancing research, education, and innovation across member institutions. The Innovators Award recognises projects with cross-border potential and genuine interdisciplinary ambition.
