## Falling Walls Lab Wrocław

Falling Walls Lab Wrocław brings together early-stage projects ready to be presented beyond their internal circles. The event is known for highlighting ideas with potential for further development across research, culture, and innovation.

**Living Chess appeared there for the first time in a public setting.**

### The presentation

The presentation introduced:

- The project's core logic — one player, one piece, decisions made collectively
- The **role-based structure** where piece rank determines voting weight
- The three-stage decision cycle that separates suggestion, curation, and resolution
- The direction of the work that would follow: testplays, research, and platform development

### What it opened

Taking part placed the concept in a visible environment and opened its trajectory outside private discussions. It was a moment of going from *internal clarity* to *public proposition* — testing whether the idea was legible to audiences who hadn't been part of building it.

The answer was yes.
