Technical infrastructure

TechLab

The systems, architecture, and experiments that power Living Chess — from the online game platform to XR prototypes and AI agent research.

Platform

The online game platform

The platform at game.livingchess.net runs the full Living Chess decision cycle in real time — suggestion, curation, weighted vote, and move execution — for live sessions with distributed teams.

Real-time sessions

Live game cycles with role-based access — each participant controls exactly one piece, with weighted voting reflected instantly across all clients.

Weighted decision engine

Every vote carries piece-rank weight. The engine tallies suggestions in real time and resolves each cycle deterministically.

Session logging

Full game records — every suggestion, curation cut, vote, and outcome — stored for research analysis and replay.

Spatial computing

XR & immersive prototypes

Living Chess extends beyond screens. We prototype immersive versions of the game in 3D and XR environments, exploring how spatial presence changes group decision-making dynamics.

3D board prototypes

Interactive 3D representations of the chessboard built by Asmar Khalilli, placing participants in a spatial decision environment.

Research contexts

Deployed in academic and theatre settings to observe how physical embodiment and spatial awareness alter collective behaviour.

AI Research

Agent experiments & AI systems

We run Living Chess with autonomous AI agents to study how synthetic groups converge — or fracture — under the same three-stage decision mechanic that human teams use.
